Privacy Policy for Oratio

Last updated: May 7, 2026

Oratio is a rosary prayer app for Apple Watch developed by Jakub Florczak.

Data Collection

Oratio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app has no internet connection, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs.

Data Stored Locally

The app stores the following data exclusively on your device using UserDefaults:

  • Current bead position in the rosary
  • Selected bead style
  • Selected haptic feedback settings
  • Swipe direction preference
  • Language preference

This data never leaves your device and is never shared with anyone.

Third Parties

Oratio does not share any data with third parties. No advertising networks, analytics services, or data brokers are used.

Children's Privacy

Oratio does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
